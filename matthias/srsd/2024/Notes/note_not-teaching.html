what we fail to teach:

Rarely, though, are the learning goals stated explicitly. Plus, the intense focus on programming obscures what a university course can, and should, contribute to the programming education at this stage of a student’s education.

——————————————————————

Turn this into an instructor note.

What can academia contribute to the preparation of software developers?

What we cannot do.

We are in no position to introduce students to the latest industry tools. Too much diversity of languages and tools. Too low level. Changes too quickly.

What we can do.

Equip them with a mindset and basic technical skills that support this mind set.
