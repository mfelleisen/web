6.2 Switching LanguagesðŸ”—

1. Make sure that both partners wish to switch. (I have experienced cases of “coercion” or “I don’t know better”.)

2. Here are the questions to ask:

— Is X a language problem or a tool chain problem? [Make sure they understand that difference, too. They may end up in the same tool chain.]

— If X is your major problem, do you know that a switch from PL 1 to PL 2 is going to eliminate X?

— Can you re-do problems B, C, D, and E in a truly exploratory manner for PL 2 now that you know what we think “exploratory” means? Can you at least make sure that these other requirements don’t cause new problems in PL 2?

If they can rationalize answers for these questions, put them into the PL 2 language pool and let Cameron know.

If not, revisit 1 and check whether there’s something else going on. Perhaps it’s a question of splitting them up and letting each of them choose separately.
