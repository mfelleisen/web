1 Roles

Ben

Ben Greenman will manage the github accounts for the students in the three sections, run the test fests, and collect the grades (automatically from the test fests and grade reports sent to students).

Each homework requires the following actions:

  • on the morning after the due date, push out the self evaluation for the assignment;

  • also on the morning, harvest the solutions from the pairs;

  • check the "delivery" and "executable" part of the test fest.

  • start running the test fest;

  • 24 hours later after sending out the self-evaluations, git-pull just the those and assign each head and all graders a certain number of student github accounts to grade.

  • ideally each pair of students should get results from the test fest within 48 to 72 hours in a directory in the specific homework directory.

The head of each section will assist the instructor with conducting code walks: managing questions from on-line participants, observing the presenters and the panelists, making suggestions, and correcting the instructor (if needed).

The shoulder attends too and "shoulders" some of the work of the head, especially observing the presenters and the panelists. It might be best to take notes. After the lecture, the instructor, the section head, and shoulder meet and evaluate the code walks.
