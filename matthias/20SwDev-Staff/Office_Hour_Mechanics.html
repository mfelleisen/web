4 Office Hour Mechanics

Thanks to Kanika and Julia for their help with this protocol.

Each pod leader should pick two hours per week; each grader should set up one hour for now. If demand picks up or slackens, we will change this set up.

To manage these office hour slots, follow this procedure:

  1. Make sure to set up your Northeastern Zoom account.

    Sign in using Northeastern single sign-on.

  2. In Zoom, set up a recurring meeting and enable the "waiting room" option.

    When one pair of students needs more than the 15 minutes, the next one will land in the waiting room until you invite the two into the personal room.

  3. Set up an account on Calendly.

    Calendly manages appointments for you. It is integrated with calendars and thus provides automatic reminders.

  4. With Calendly, set up 15-minute slots that match your office hours, recurring on a weekly basis.

    This prohibits students from booking outside of your chosen hours.  We will change to shorter or longer time slots as demand evolves.

  5. In the card details of events on Calendly, update Zoom meeting room details in the description. Make sure to include password information if needed.

    When students book slots on your available hours, they get the Zoom link details via mail. No action required on your side.

Send your Calendly link to Matthias. The links will be attached to your names so that students can easily book an appointment.

Watch Kanika’s video for details.
