—Your Favorite PL

Due Friday, September 11, by the end of your first lecture

Purpose read emails to your Northeastern address and react to them appropriately; live up to task specifications

You will receive 10 points for timely delivery of the email and another 10 points for a correctly formatted email.

Task Send email to the technical TA with the following content:

Subject: CS 4500

your last name
your first name
the last four digits of your NU student id
your CCIS usernames
most direct way for your partner to contact you your chosen language

a free-form rationale for your choice of up to 140 characters

Requirements The programming language of your choice must support a number of services on the delivery system: access to command-line arguments, the standard input/output devices, a JSON library, a rudimentary GUI library, and network connectivity.

Response In response you will receive an email that

  • the name of a partner who has committed to use the same programming language;

  • the direct contact information for your partner; and

  • assigns the two of you a git repository on the College’s Enterprise Github system.

You are to contact your partner immediately on receipt of this message and coordinate a first (on-line) meeting.
