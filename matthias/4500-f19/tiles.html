The 35

A tile-pat is [tile-index, degree].

Here is a pictorial rendering of the 35 unique tile configurations. All tiles are the result of rotating one of these tiles by 0, 90, 180, or 270 degrees clockwise. Instead of data representations of tiles, our project will use the index on the left to designate a unique tile configuration, plus a rotation.
