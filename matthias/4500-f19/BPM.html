BPM — Post Mortem

Due Sunday, September 15, midnight

Some of you will receive notification about problems with your delivery of TAHBPL part B, namely,

  • B not found in directory B/

  • B found but the executable bit is not set

  • B found, executable bit set, but the program does not accept STDIN input

If so, you will lose all points for Task 1 of Assignment B unless you fix the problem by the specified due date and write an explanatory note to your team leader, Michael Ballantyne.

The note must explain in a single paragraph what you did wrong.

Delivery You must send the note in email to your team leader by the specified deadline after fixing your github repository in accordance with the memo. Your team leader will act on your note and attempt to work with your artifacts a second time.

If this second try does not succeed, you do lose all points.
