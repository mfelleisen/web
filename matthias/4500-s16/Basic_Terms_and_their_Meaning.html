Basic Terms and their Meaning

A watering hole board is the place where food tokens becomes and remains available.

Food bags help players gather food tokens.

Each of the some 120 trait cards plays two roles. On one hand, it describes an attribute of a species, for example, whether and how it can defend itself against attacks from Carnivores. On the other hand, it represents a certain number of food tokens.

A species board represents a species, specifically its population size and the body size of the animals. Both population and body size range between 0 and 7.
